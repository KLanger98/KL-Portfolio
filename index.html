About Me.

Hello, My name is Karl Langer.

I am a (soon to be) Fullstack Developer based in Adelaide, Australia. I am currently completing the Adelaide University Web Development Bootcamp and have been teaching myself to code over the last year and a half as a hobby.

My current expertise lies in FrontEnd languages and Javascript with a keen interest to explore the backend.
